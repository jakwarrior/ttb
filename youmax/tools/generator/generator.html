Video Display Mode Video Display Mode -
1. Popup - show videos in a popup
2. Inline Top - show videos inside the Youmax widget at the top
3. Inline Thumbnail - show videos inside the video thumbnail itself
4. Link to YouTube - show videos on YouTube page in a new Tab
5. New Page - show videos on a new page in your website with a different link

Video Protocol Protocol under which videos will be displayed -
1. HTTP
2. HTTPS - for SSL websites

Featured Video Link to a YouTube video that will be displayed in the player when the Youmax widget loads. Works only for 'Inline' display mode.

Use separate links for videos Shows separate links for each video. Works only for 'New Page' display mode.

Video Size For very wide displays, video player width can be set to wide or narrow. Works only for 'Inline' or 'New Page' display mode.

AutoPlay video when thumbnail is clicked Normally user will need to click on the big Play button on the Video player. With this option, video will start playing automatically when a thumbnail is clicked.

Display first video on page load Displays the first video automatically in the Video player when Youmax loads or a new Tab is clicked. Works only for 'Inline' display mode.

Playlist Action When a playlist thumbnail is clicked -
1. Show all Video Thumbnails in Youmax
2. Play the complete playlist in the Video player

Video Player Theme Video player theme can be 'dark' or 'light'. Works only for YouTube player.

Show Title above (with) Video Player Video player will display title at the top. Works only for YouTube player.

Display comments automatically Normally a user will have to click on 'Show Comments' button to display comments under the Video player. Setting this option will display comments automatically below the video player.
